Value Added Service Beyond Test Results

As a third-party laboratory, Tri Geosynthetic Testing and Services (Suzhou) Co., Ltd. is continuously seeking for excellence. We offer trustworthy services including, geosynthetic testing, training, material conformity certification, leak detection, and construction quality assurance.

TRI Geosynthetic Testing and Services (Suzhou) Co., Ltd. was originally known as Precision Geosynthetic Laboratories-Suzhou (PGL-Suzhou) owned by Precision Geosythetic Laboratories International (PGLI). PGLI, established in 1984, is located in California U.S.A. in January 2012, Texas Research International, Inc. (TRI, Inc.) officially acquired both PGLI Laboratories in U.S.A and China. This incorporation provided the group with further technical resources and expertise.

Full Service Geosynthetics Laboratory

TRI Geosynthetic Testing and Services (Suzhou) Co., Ltd. {or TRI Suzhou} functions as an independent testing laboratory. Similar to TRI-California and TRI-Texas (TRI Laboratories), TRI-Suzhou is envisioned to carry out a full range of Geosynthetic Testing Services; namely, conformance, seam, direct shear and compatibility testing of all geosynthetic materials such as: geosynthetic clay liner (GCL), geomembrane (VC, RPP, fRPP, HDPE, LLDPE, Hypalon, Hytrel, EPDM, fPP, chlorinated PE, Butyl Rubber, asphalt/bitumen, ABS, etc.), geotextile, geocomposite, geonet, geogrid and plastic pipes. We will also be equipped to perform testing of various erosion control products.

TRI-Suzhou can perform various geosynthetic testing in accordance with ASTM, EN, ISO, BS, DIN, GRI, Australian standards, etc. If the client so requires, our TRI main laboratories in the US can perform specialized interface friction, permeability, creep and stress-rupture, transmissivity , gradient ratio, UV-resistance, chemical resistance, and accelerated time-temperature creep and stress rupture testing. The TRI US laboratories perform more than 117 GAI-LAP accredited testing procedures.

Leak Detection – Liner Integrity Surveys / Assessments (LISA)

Lisa technologies developed by TRI – Texas can be utilized to swiftly non-destructively detect small holes or leaks in geomembrane liner systems. This process can effectively locate construction-related damages incurred in the liners of Landfills, water/wastewater reservoirs, and other similar facilities. As such, LISA is an important aspect of CQA to ensure early detection of material defects or construction-related leaks. Hole/leaks repair works implemented during construction phase are more economical versus repair works performed in the operating stage of a facility. TRI-Suzhou also offers LISA field services.

In Plant Sampling and Monitoring:

TRI US laboratories are the industry’s leaders in providing fast, accurate and cost-effective In-Plant Monitoring and Sampling program to our customers. We offer Manufacturing Quality Assurance (MQA) In-Plant sampling services in all major geosynthetics production plants worldwide including: Us, France, Canada, Thailand, Hong Kong, Taiwan, Malaysia and china. TRI-Suzhou performs In-Plant Sampling and Monitoring in major geosynthetic manufacturers in Asia. TRI laboratories are independent of any facility owner, manufacturer, contractor, installer, CQA and engineering/consulting firm. MQA involves the following tasks:

  1. Plant Visitation

    TRI inspector goes to the manufacturer to witness and observe the startup of production

  2. Audit QA/QC of the manufacturer

    TRI reviews the manufacturing QA/QC Procedures of the manufacturer

  3. Help establish QC for manufacturers’ product

    If the manufacturer lacks of the QC program or the QC program is inadequate and/or inappropriate, TRI can establish or improve it.

  4. Monitor production

    TRI inspector will monitor production and reviews QC data on a daily basis or as in frequencies required by the client.

  5. Sampling

    TRI selects the sample rolls or specifies the sampling frequency and the manufacturer cuts the samples and sends them to TRI-Suzhou laboratory

    TRI-Suzhou inspector is present during the sampling and verifies the roll from which the sample was taken.
